SCOBY is an indie/alternative rock outfit from Winston-Salem, NC. While shifting band mem- bers, the homegrown group recorded their first EP "That Will Be The End" in their drummer Doug's basement. It couples a DIY indie ethic with sentimental themes of grief, growth, and coming into identity. It functions as a diary of young adulthood, featuring jittery hooks and composition that is "uninhibited and confident- ly playful (CLTure Magazine). Since its release, SCOBY has made waves touring across the Car- olinas, being compared to acts such as Soccer Mommy, Snail Mail, and Wednesday. Their up- coming LP features singles "You Scare Me Like A Movie" and "PT Cruiser"
